Pathway students who choose to continue their education on campus after Pathway will pay regular on-campus tuition.

Financial Aid

Financial aid is not available for Pathway students. However, U.S. students who have a high school diploma or GED may apply for federal financial aid after they complete Pathway and if they are accepted into a degree-seeking program.
